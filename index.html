About Us

Welcome to Fruli, the newest photogenic spot on Dago Street, West Java!

Fruli brings the vibrant flavors of fruits to life with fresh drinks and fruits, all crafted to brighten your day. Perfectly located and designed for both taste and aesthetic lovers, Fruli is your new go-to for refreshing fruits and colorful moments. Visit us and experience the fresh side of Dago!

Long before the first stone cities rose and empires etched their names in history, humanity discovered a quiet alchemy — the fermentation of grains into a golden elixir. In clay pots buried in the earth, early civilizations found not only sustenance but celebration, culture, and craft. Beer was born from the land, transformed by time, and passed down by hands that understood the magic of patience.

Across Mesopotamia, Egypt, and Europe, this sacred brew evolved — refined by monks in abbeys and experimented upon in bustling market towns. Each era added its signature, each region a flavor, each brewer a story. What began as a humble act of preservation became an enduring tradition of joy and gathering.

At FRULI, we are heirs to that tradition. We honor the ancient roots of beer-making while forging our own path — blending the time-honored art of fermentation with bold, fruit-forward innovation. Our brews are more than beverages; they are a bridge between the past and present, crafted for those who savor authenticity and flavor in equal measure. This is the journey of beer. This is the spirit of FRULI.
